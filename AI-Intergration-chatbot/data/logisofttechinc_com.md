Job Description
Technical M&A Platform Manager
, the person in this role will support all technical aspects of M&A Systems implementations and programmes. This role will have accountability for reviewing technical solutions and vendor impact assessments proposed by vendors.
Key Responsibilities:
M&A Strategy Integration:
Collaborate with IT leadership to understand the company’s M&A strategy and objectives.
Develop and implement a comprehensive plan to integrate EVO solutions into newly acquired entities.
Product Management:
Oversee the entire technical aspects, ensuring alignment with business goals and industry best practices.
Provide expertise on SAP integration and ABAP programming.
Project Leadership:
Lead and manage end-to-end technical aspects of M&A projects, ensuring timelines and budgets are met.
Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including IT, FIN, SCM and HR, to drive project success.
Stakeholder Engagement:
Engage with key stakeholders to understand their requirements and concerns related to technical aspects.
Provide regular updates to leadership on project progress, challenges, and successes.
Change Management:
Develop and execute change management strategies to ensure a smooth transition during M&A activities.
Conduct training sessions and provide ongoing support to end-users.
Vendor Management:
Work closely with SAP vendors to stay informed about updates, enhancements, and new features.
Evaluate and recommend additional IT solutions that align with the company’s evolving needs.
Expectations:
We are looking for a trusted and motivated IT Technical Product manager who can effectively develop and deliver the Portfolio vision, consulting the business and is passionate for innovation.
The candidate will:
have deep technical, hands-on knowledge of SAP integration development using SAP PI including use of ABAP proxies IDOC’s and RFCs. Experience in designing a PI solution based on a functional requirement.
have experience working on SOAP and REST APIs
have good understanding of cloud solutions and new technologies.
have a understanding of SAP functional areas.
Have a minimum of 8 to 10 years of experience.
deliver projects and initiatives in an agile/SAFe manner – driving a product backlog with business alignment, so that we ensure delivery across matrix teams and challenge vendors to address our priorities.
serve as the key technology’s point of contact to ensure effective support, advice and expertise on the design and best practice to exploit value from the product(s) the individual leads.
produce and review end to end architecture for Mergers, acquisition, divestiture, and Joint ventures.
lead a team of internal and external suppliers to deliver complex M&A projects.
ensure compliancy with security, architecture, privacy/GDPR, SOX and audit controls.
Key accountabilities:
They will assist and advise on the target architecture vision, evaluating and supporting the prioritisation of business requirements, translating them into designs, principles, changes, and non-functional requirements.
Represent Technical IT towards Business functions, System Integrators, M&A Projects, and current Live Markets solutions
Manage the cross Functional requirements impacting EVO, touchpoints with other EVO and local market systems
Review and approval of Global and Local Technical enhancement and improvements, including further integrations & data migration approach
Responsible for designing and developing state-of-the-art reusable solutions, implement new technologies to drive global scalability, revenues and cost efficiencies throughout Vodafone Group
Key contributor for the innovation of IT systems
Represent Technical cross functions team for everything related to M&A
Manage the cross Functional review of requirements covering all technical aspects
Manage the validation process for functional requirements from a technical perspective
Review and approval of Global/Local Technical Enterprise Architecture, including further integrations & Data Migration approach
General skills, knowledge and experience:
Essential
Deep knowledge of SAP PI / PO / CPI / APIs
Knowledge of ABAP proxies, Idocs, RFCs
Ability to translate customer needs into technical requirements.
Understanding of integration architecture and patterns including BTP technologies
Experience in managing a sophisticated customer environment, where Tools and Automation are key to enable the continuous transformation/evolution of the organization and the constant tension to efficiency.
Capability in managing technical aspects of large projects.
Strong communication and negotiation skills
Can do attitude.
Desired
SAP Techno-Functional knowledge
ABAP Knowledge
Understanding of Agile (SAFe ) and Waterfall project management methodologies
